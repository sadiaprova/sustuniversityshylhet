Welcome to SUST

Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST) is a leading public research university in Bangladesh dedicated to providing world-class scientific and technological education and research.

Our Mission

To foster knowledge and research in science and technology for the advancement of society and to develop skilled professionals who can contribute effectively to national and global progress.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in science, technology, and innovation recognized globally for academic leadership and pioneering research.

Academic Excellence

At SUST, we provide a comprehensive academic environment with diverse programs, experienced faculty, and modern facilities that nurture intellectual curiosity and creativity.
