About the Department

Our Mission

To educate and train students in the principles and practices of industrial and production engineering, preparing them to design, improve, and manage integrated systems of people, materials, information, equipment, and energy.

Our Facilities

  • ✓ Modern manufacturing and automation labs
  • ✓ Computer-aided design and simulation facilities
  • ✓ Ergonomics and human factors research center
  • ✓ Collaborations with leading industries

Why Choose Industrial & Production Engineering?

Industrial Engineering

Focus on optimizing complex processes and systems to eliminate waste of time, money, materials, and energy.

Production Engineering

Specialized training in manufacturing processes, automation, and production system design.

Systems Approach

Holistic approach to problem-solving that considers all aspects of a system and its interactions.
