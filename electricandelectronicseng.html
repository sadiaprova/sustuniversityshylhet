About the Department

Our History

Established in 1993, the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at SUST has been at the forefront of technological education in Bangladesh. Our mission is to produce skilled engineers equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience to meet the demands of the evolving technological landscape.

Our Facilities

  • ✓ State-of-the-art laboratories with modern equipment
  • ✓ Advanced research facilities in power systems and electronics
  • ✓ Industry-standard software and simulation tools
  • ✓ Collaborations with leading tech companies and research institutions

Why Choose EEE at SUST?

Cutting-Edge Curriculum

Our continuously updated curriculum covers power systems, electronics, telecommunications, and computer engineering with hands-on training.

Research Opportunities

Students engage in innovative research projects focusing on renewable energy, IoT, signal processing, and power electronics.

Industry Connections

Strong ties with national electrical companies, power stations, and tech firms providing internship and job opportunities.
