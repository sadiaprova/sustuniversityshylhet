About the Department

Our Mission

To produce highly skilled food engineers and tea technologists capable of meeting the challenges of food processing, preservation, and tea technology industries through quality education, research, and innovation.

Our Facilities

  • ✓ Modern food processing and tea processing laboratories
  • ✓ Sensory evaluation and quality control labs
  • ✓ Pilot plant for food and tea processing
  • ✓ Collaborations with leading food and tea industries

Why Choose Food Engineering & Tea Technology?

Food Engineering

Comprehensive training in food processing, preservation, packaging, and quality control with hands-on experience in modern facilities.

Tea Technology

Specialized training in tea cultivation, processing, blending, and quality evaluation with direct industry exposure.

Research Focus

Cutting-edge research in food safety, functional foods, tea chemistry, and sustainable food processing technologies.
