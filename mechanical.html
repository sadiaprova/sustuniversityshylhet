About the Department

Our Mission

To provide world-class education in mechanical engineering, fostering innovation and research in design, thermal systems, manufacturing, and materials science to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Facilities

  • ✓ State-of-the-art machine shops and fabrication labs
  • ✓ Advanced CAD/CAM/CAE facilities
  • ✓ Thermodynamics and fluid mechanics research center
  • ✓ Collaborations with leading engineering firms

Why Choose Mechanical Engineering?

Diverse Specializations

Opportunities in automotive, aerospace, energy, robotics, and manufacturing industries with broad career prospects.

Hands-on Learning

Extensive laboratory work, workshops, and design projects that bridge theory with practical applications.

Innovation Focus

Emphasis on creative problem-solving, design thinking, and developing sustainable engineering solutions.
