About Our Department

Our History

Established in 1991, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SUST has been at the forefront of technological education in Bangladesh. We were one of the first universities in the country to offer a full-fledged CSE program.

Our Mission

To produce world-class computer science graduates equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. We aim to foster innovation, research, and ethical computing practices to serve the nation and the global community.
