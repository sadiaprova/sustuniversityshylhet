About Our Department

Our Mission

To educate future civil and environmental engineers who will design sustainable infrastructure, solve environmental challenges, and contribute to the nation's development while preserving natural resources for future generations.

Program Highlights

  • Four-year B.Sc. in Civil Engineering with specialization options
  • M.Sc. and Ph.D. programs in various specializations
  • Strong industry-academia collaboration for practical training
  • Focus on sustainable and climate-resilient infrastructure

Department History

Established in 1993, the Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering at SUST has grown to become one of the premier engineering departments in Bangladesh. We have produced thousands of graduates who are now serving in key positions nationally and internationally.
