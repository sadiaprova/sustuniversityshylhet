SUST University Research

At Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, research excellence is the cornerstone of our mission. We foster innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and solutions to global challenges through cutting-edge research initiatives.

Explore our research centers, publications, and opportunities designed to empower researchers, students, and partners.

Research Centers & Labs

Center for Advanced Materials Research

Engages in innovative research on nanomaterials, composites, and sustainable materials for industry and academia.

Institute of Environmental Studies

Focused on environmental policy, climate change, and sustainable development research with community engagement.

Robotics and Intelligent Systems Lab

Dedicated to AI-driven robotics, automation technologies, and intelligent systems research for real-world applications.

Renewable Energy Research Center

Innovating in solar, wind, and alternative energy technologies to promote sustainable energy solutions.
